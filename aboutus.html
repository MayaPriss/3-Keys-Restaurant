ABOUT US


3 KEYs began with a professional badminton player turned ambitious young chef, Lee Chong Jia. Born in 1993, he discovered his talent for cooking at the young age of 11. It wasn't until after he graduated from Taylor's University with a diploma in culinary arts where he found his love for Malaysian food. Lee Chong Jia was a man who was very friendly and has always wanted nothing but peace and unity for the country. He had the idea of opening up a restaurant which would enable any diners to experience all the marvelous food available in Malaysia. With this idea in mind, he opened up 3 KEYs on September 6th, 2020.


HALAL POLICY


The halal food complies with Islamic Law or Shariah. Halal stipulates that food consumed must be:
  • Hygienic
  • Not harmful to health
  • Free from any parts of animal origin that is forbidden by the shariah.
  • Free from anything regarded as 'filth'(najis) under Islamic Law.

In addition, meat products must be derived from animals that are permissible and slaughtered according to Islamic Law.

The products that are used in 3 KEYs are certified Halal by the source country local Islamic food and nutrition certification recognised by JAKIM.

Our restaurant is 100% halal.

The foods served on our menu are 100% pork-free and comply with Halal requirements in relation to the cooking process.


QUALITY ASSURANCE


Our restaurant hygiene and food quality are always top-notch as we want to provide our customers with high quality products.

The performance of the supplier for our restaurant are monitored through submission of quality reports.

Our restaurant also goes by the main attribute for excellence - CHAMPS.

  • C - Cleanliness
  • H - Hospitality
  • A - Accuracy
  • M - Maintenance
  • P - Product Quality
  • S - Speed of Service

This CHAMPS attribute covers all aspects such as cleanliness and maintenance, sanitation and personal hygiene as well as product quality. It also takes into account the speed of service, hospitality, personal safety, training and documentation.

As much as our restaurant is top-notch on the food and hygiene department, we also want to ensure the customer service provided are top-notch.

To gather direct feedback from customers, an online survey program is used. Here, customers can rate and comment on the cleanliness, staff friendliness, speed of service, order accuracy, food taste and overall satisfaction.

This gives room to our restaurant for continuous improvement and makes sure our customers are always satisfied.
